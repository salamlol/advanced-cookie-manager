Advanced Cookie Manager does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

All cookie management, encryption, and settings are processed locally within the user’s browser using Chrome Extension APIs.

No data is sent to external servers. No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used.

Any data stored (such as settings or optional passwords) remains on the user’s device and can be removed at any time by uninstalling the extension.
